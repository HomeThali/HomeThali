Dosa

Crispy on the outside, fluffy on the inside—a true South Indian classic.

Savor the authentic taste of fermented rice and lentil batter, cooked to perfection. Served with creamy coconut chutney and tangy sambar, this is a wholesome and satisfying meal for any time of the day.

Idli

Soft, fluffy, and healthy—Idli is a timeless breakfast favorite.

Steamed to perfection, these rice cakes are light on the stomach and packed with nutrition. Pair them with sambar and a variety of chutneys for a hearty start to your day.

Full Meals

A wholesome experience with a variety of flavors on one platter.

Enjoy a complete South Indian thali featuring rice, curry, sambar, chutney, and more. Every bite offers a burst of flavors that showcase the essence of traditional Indian cuisine.

Poori Sagu

Golden, puffed Pooris served with a flavorful potato curry.

Indulge in this comforting combination of crispy deep-fried bread paired with a mildly spiced potato masala. It's a classic dish that brings warmth and satisfaction in every bite.

Pulao

Aromatic, flavorful, and loaded with veggies or meat.

Pulao is a delightful rice dish cooked with fragrant spices and wholesome ingredients. Whether you prefer a vegetable medley or tender chunks of meat, Pulao is a feast for your taste buds.
